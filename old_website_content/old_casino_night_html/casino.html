Casino Night is a department-wide formal for computing majors (and their friends of any major or gender) hosted by the Association for Women in Computing (AWC). This event will be held on the ground floor of the Brendan Iribe Center for Computer Science and Engineering on April 18th, 2024 at 5:30 PM to 9:30 PM.

We have casino games dealt by your favorite computing faculty, opportunities to network with our corporate sponsors, dinner, raffle prizes, and more! Casino Night was created nine years ago as a way for computing student to mingle with each other and sponsors. To honor the 9th year of Casino Night, our theme is Breakfast at Tiffany's, inspired by the timeless film, which promises a blend of classic glamor and excitement. We hope to see you there!
