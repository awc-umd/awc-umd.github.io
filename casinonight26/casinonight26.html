Association for Women In Computing Presents

CASINO NIGHT (2026)

16 APRIL | 5:30 - 9:30 PM | Brendan Iribe Center Lobby

What is Casino Night?

Casino Night is AWC’s annual spring formal for students, faculty, and sponsors to meet and mingle! Dress up in your best formal attire, network with our corporate sponsors, eat some dinner, and more! If you have what it takes, play casino games dealt by your favorite faculty and enter into the raffles for a chance to win prizes!

Casino Night started 11 years ago as a way for CS students and friends to meet with each other and sponsors. In honor of our 11th year, our theme is: Clue (1985)! Inspired by the classic thriller, we ensure a night of memories, mystery, and more!


Casino Night is a department wide formal for computing majors (and their friends of any major or gender) hosted by the Association of Women in Computing. The event will be held on the ground floor lobby of the Brendan Iribe Center for Computer Science and Engineering on April 16th, 2026, from 5:30 to 9:30 PM.

Tickets include 1 free drink, a meal, games, and a night of action!
