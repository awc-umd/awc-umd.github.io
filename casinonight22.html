Mission Details


Casino Night is a department-wide formal for computing majors (and their friends of any major or gender) hosted by the Association for Women in Computing (AWC). This event will be held on the ground floor of the Brendan Iribe Center for Computer Science and Engineering on April 21st, 2022 at 6-9pm. We have casino games dealt by your favorite computing faculty, opportunities to network with our corporate sponsors, dinner, raffle prizes, and more!

Casino Night was created seven years ago as a way for computing student to mingle with each other and sponsors. After a few years of hiatus we are bringing back this tried-and-true AWC tradition. To honor the 7th year of Casino Night, our theme is 007, inspired by James Bond cinematography.

We hope to see you there!
