Lights, Camera, Awc!

You are cordially invited to Casino Night, an annual event held by the Association for Women in Computing (AWC). To honor the 10th year of AWC Casino Night, this year's theme is: Hollywood Red Carpet! Bring your glamour and glitter for a fantastic night with even better company.

Casino Night is a department-wide formal for computing majors (and their friends of any major or gender) hosted by the Association for Women in Computing. This event will be held on the ground floor of the Brendan Iribe Center for Computer Science and Engineering on April 25th, 2025 at 5:30 PM to 9:30 PM.

Tickets include 1 free drink, a meal, games, and a night of Hollywood action.
