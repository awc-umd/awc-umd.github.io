We Request the Pleasure of Your Company at AWC Casino Night


Casino Night is a department-wide formal for computing majors (and their friends of any major or gender) hosted by the Association for Women in Computing (AWC). This event will be held on the ground floor of the Brendan Iribe Center for Computer Science and Engineering on April 20th, 2023 at 5:30 PM to 9:30 PM.

We have casino games dealt by your favorite computing faculty, opportunities to network with our corporate sponsors, dinner, raffle prizes, and more! Casino Night was created eight years ago as a way for computing student to mingle with each other and sponsors.

To honor the 8th year of Casino Night, our theme is Ocean's 8, inspired by Ocean's 8 cinematography and the Met Gala. We hope to see you there!
